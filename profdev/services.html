Navigating your career as a CS student can be tough. Having a friend who's been through it all can help tremendously. UPE offers all of the following as free services to the Berkeley student community.

All Professional Services appointments are at most 50 minutes and start on Berkeley Time.

Our Professional Development committee members provide advising in a number of areas. Read about your Professional Mentor here.

{% include section-header.html title="Appointment Types" %}
  1. Résumé Reviews - A personalized critique of your résumé.
  2. Mock Interviews - Technical questions and coding problems followed by feedback.
  3. Career Mentorship - Ask questions and learn from our experience in teaching, research, and internships.
  4. Academic Advising - Ask course-content related questions and create a plan for Berkeley CS.
